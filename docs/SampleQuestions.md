# Sample Questions

To help you get started, here are some **Sample Questions** you can ask in the **Explore** chat experience after loading a scenario. The questions are grouped by the built-in sample scenario packs. If you uploaded your own data, adapt these questions to your dataset.

## Contact Center

1. Please provide the total number of calls by date for the last 7 days.
2. Provide a summary of performance issues users reported this week.
3. What are the most common customer complaints?
4. Turn these key topics into a structured FAQ.
5. Which agents handled the most calls, and what were the outcomes?

## Telecom Analysis

1. Total number of calls by date for the last 7 days.
2. What are the top 7 challenges users reported?
3. What are the top recommendations to reduce these customer challenges?
4. Summarize the sentiment breakdown across all recorded calls.
5. Which topics appear most frequently in the call recordings?

## Mortgage Application

1. What are the key findings in the Annual Housing Report?
2. What does the report say about accessibility in housing?
3. Summarize the key clauses in the purchase contracts.
4. What risks are identified across the mortgage documents?

## Bring Your Own Data

If you connected an Azure AI Search index or a Microsoft Fabric warehouse, ask questions relevant to your fields, for example:

1. Summarize the main themes in my data.
2. What are the top categories by volume?
3. Show the trend over time for the key metric in my dataset.

## Tips for Better Answers

- Be specific about time ranges (e.g., "last 7 days", "this month").
- Ask follow-up questions — the chat is multi-turn and retains context.
- Use the **Insights** page to view auto-generated KPIs and charts alongside the chat.
